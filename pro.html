FAQ

Questions

Is this a finished, shipping product?

Not yet. We're validating demand and finishing supplier sample testing. Your $150 deposit reserves your spot and locks in founding member pricing ($2,886 total) — it does not commit you to buy. You'll pay the remaining $2,736 only once production is confirmed, and can request a full refund of your deposit any time before that.

Is MOEV Pro powered?

Yes. Unlike the original passive Moev, MOEV Pro uses a battery-powered actuator system with real-time sensing to provide active lift assistance, not just passive strap support.

How long does the battery last?

An estimated 8–10 hours of use per charge, with a full recharge in around 3 hours. Final figures will be confirmed after sample testing.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes, in full, any time before we confirm the production run and charge the remaining balance. Just email us.

How is this different from the original Moev?

Moev is a lightweight, passive, motor-free design. MOEV Pro is a heavier-duty, battery-powered, actuated design for teams that need more active load support. Both are available to reserve — pick whichever fits your use case, or reserve both.

Who's behind MOEV Pro?

MOEV Pro is an early-stage product being developed and tested by No Agents Pty Ltd, the makers of Moev. Questions? Email us at alex.s.bourne@gmail.com.
